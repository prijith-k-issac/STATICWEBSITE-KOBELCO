FEATURED VIDEO

The first demolition machine in
the Benelux, the SK400DLC-10

Van Groningen Sloopwerken B.V. has successfully
demolished 42 houses in Maassluis (the
Netherlands) and took care of the remediation, the
manual preliminary demolition and the total
demolition. It was an elongated housing block in
which the residents and the environment had to be
taken into account. All material flows, with the
exception of the mixed rubble, were removed
separately. They broke the rubble into mixed
granulate on site. Spraying was carried out to keep
the dust emission to a minimum.
